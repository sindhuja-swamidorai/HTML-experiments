My Favourite vacation destinations

Wayanad in Kerala is one of my favourite vacation destination. It provides a range of options including trekking, boating, wildlife safari etc.


Bada in Coastal Karnataka is famous for its beaches


Tiruchendur, the temple city of TamilNadu is one of my favourite destination to spend with the entire family. If provides peace to both mind and body. The sea surrounding the temple also provides for some fun and play.


The Golden Gate Bridge in Sanfrancisco, USA is one of my favourite foreign destinations.
