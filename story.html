Surprise Visit

Sneak Peak

Reema was expecting a visitor who would turn her birthday special.
Did the visitor come or someone else?

The Story

Reema had invited her friends for her birthday party. After the initial celebrations
she had plans of a late night party with some of her close friends. She was expecting
her fiancee to join for the late night party and made all arrangements accordingly.

On the special day, her parents visited her in the morning to give her a surprise and
they planned to stay for a day. Reema had to cancel her plans of the late night party
and celebrated her birthday with her parents and friends.
